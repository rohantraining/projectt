Be prepared for three miles of relatively easy and scenic hiking. If you love wildlife, you’ll likely agree that this is one of the best places to hike in the world. Getting Here From NYC: If you are planning on doing this hike from NYC, you’ll want to take a train from Penn Station to the last stop in Montauk. From the train station, it’s a 15-minute taxi/Uber to the trail. Tour Option From NYC: You can also book this private tour from NYC that includes visiting Montauk!.

You can see them both from the beachfront and also from a nearby observation tower after you have completed the trail to Montauk Point. The best time to visit is from the late fall to early spring, as this offers your best chances at seeing seals.
